Who We Are


We at Sapphire Youngsters TV are a seasoned host of community driven by the passion of achieving academic and social excellence in our leaders of tommorow.

Our team is made up of passionate, gifted and talented individuals; all knowledgable in their different fields of contribution and expertise; put together by the CEO and Chairman Mr. Alex Dada Linus, we have a team dedicated to pursuing the excellence and showcasing the talents of the African child.

Mission

At Sapphire Youngsters group, we create a world-class spotlight for teens and young adults to boost their self-esteem and abilities.

Our legendary passion is to spur confidence in reaching full potentials in life. Our TV reality show spanning seven (7) to eight (8) weeks showcases unscripted real life experience starring teens between the ages 12 to 18 years of age.

We are partnering with the well-known Television giants Multichoice and several other television channels across Nigeria.
